About Us

Global Folk Arts International is dedicated to promoting and preserving the traditional arts and crafts of diverse cultures worldwide. Through symposiums, workshops, and collaborative exhibitions, we celebrate global heritage and foster creative exchange among artists. Our mission is to reunite traditional art forms from across the globe on a common platform, creating a space where artists can learn, collaborate, and share their cultural legacy with the world.
